ABOUT DML

Dar Machine Learning Group is a positive Data Science, Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning community founded in Dar es Salaam with aim to create a vibrant and diverse data scientists community in Tanzania.

Our main agenda is to organize workshop, training and summer school that aim to expose interested people especially students to artificial intelligence, data science and machine learning.

our mission

To foster the application and building youth capacity in data science, machine learning and artificial intelligence in Tanzania and promote the use of machine learning across industries, learning centers, schools and community.
